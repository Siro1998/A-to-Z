So for Pie Spacing, the rules are as follows:

1. Delete all the spaces and punctuation in the text

2. Lowercase all the letters

3. Insert a new space after every digit of pie letters (3.1415926535897932384626433832…)

- So after three letters, insert a space. And after one letter, insert another space. Then after four letters, insert a space…
